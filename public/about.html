About the Optometrist

Dr. Goodness Ngozi Nwakalor is a licensed optometrist with over 3 years of experience in providing comprehensive eye care. She graduated from Imo State University with Distinctions and has since been dedicated to helping patients maintain optimal eye health.

She specializes in contact lens fittings, pediatric eye care, and managing chronic eye conditions. She is passionate about educating her patients on the importance of regular eye exams and proper eye care practices.

In her free time, Dr. Nwakalor enjoys cooking, reading, and spending time with her family.

She is married to Dr. Chukwudi Emanuel Nwakalor, a linguist and language professor, and they have two beautiful children, Chimdiebube and Kosisochukwu.
